Background

About me

I have a long-standing passion for language learning. I started studying Japanese in 2010 and was comfortable in conversation by 2014, thanks to regular speaking practice with native speakers and consistent self-study at home. I am also fluent in French.

I am a strong advocate of spaced repetition - particularly through the Anki app - and of immersion: finding books, films, podcasts, or hobbies you genuinely enjoy in the language you are learning is the best way to stay motivated over the long term.

Outside of teaching, I work as a software engineer. You can see my professional background on LinkedIn.
